Generate a 64-bit hash aimed at maximising speed and uniformity of entropy across the 64-bit range.

Currently, it's an implementation of SipHash, and I thank those who have researched and developed SipHash, and the original designers Jean-Philippe Aumasson and Daniel J. Bernstein.

Parameters
[in]seed64 bits of seed.
[in]dataValue to be hashed.
[in]sizeSize of data.
Returns
64-bit hash value.
